satch

Greetings! My name is Herbert Brown, but you can call me 'Satch'. Amongst other things, I'm into web dev. Currently, I serve as the Sr. Instructor/Developer at the Mississippi Coding Academies.

The Mississippi Coding Academies is an 11-month, tuition-free course in the Fundamentals of Full Stack Web Development. The academy has given me a great opportunity to find a new passion in coding. Since I joined the academy, I am not only able to satisfy my passion for education, but I have also learned a skill that is fun and keeps me desiring to know more.

This portfolio page is one of the projects that we assign our codelings. I decided to redo my portfolio page. The main purpose of me doing it was to add the latest version of Bootstrap (v5.2) and quite frankly, I didn't like the way the old one looked. I want my coders to see that they can accomplish whatever they desire as long as they are willing to put in the effort and work.

This portfolio page serves to give you a little information about who I am, show some of the things I'm learning as well as teaching, a home to my youtube channel, Satchwerk, where I give short coding tutorials, and highlight another passion of mine, DJing. Feel free to explore!
